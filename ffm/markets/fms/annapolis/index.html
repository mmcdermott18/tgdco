Annapolis, MD (est. 2008)

Sundays, 8 am to noon (May 1 to Nov 20th, 2016; closed during the boat shows)

Location: Corner of Compromise St and Main St (in front of the basketball court). Click here for a map.

About This Market

If there is a better location for a farmers market in Annapolis, we don't know where it is. Situated right next to the water downtown, the market attracts both residents and tourists alike. We are incredibly fortunate to have a market where farmers have sold their products to residents for years, as our large Marion Warren photograph banner illustrates. We're proud to support farmers and producers from DE, MD (both sides of the Bay), PA and WV at this market. Stop by and shop for the freshest fruits and vegetables, baked goods, meats, eggs, cheese, flowers and more!

Contact: annapolis@freshfarmmarkets.org, Follow us

Farmers/Producers:

The most up-to-date farmer and producer lists can be found on our market map (blue button, left). Select the market date of your choice to see which farmers/producers will be at market as well as their stall assignments.
